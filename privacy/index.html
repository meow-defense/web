Privacy Policy

Meow Defense! · Effective April 7, 2026

Overview

Meow Defense! is a single-player game. Your privacy is important to me, and I have designed the app with that in mind.

Analytics

The app collects anonymous usage analytics (such as gameplay events, app version, and device type) to improve the experience. This data is processed by Amplitude and cannot be used to identify you personally. No personal data, names, emails, or accounts are collected.

Local Storage

The app stores your best score locally on your device using standard system storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Amplitude for anonymous analytics. No advertising or tracking services are used.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any personal data from anyone, including children. Analytics data is anonymous and cannot identify individual users.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
